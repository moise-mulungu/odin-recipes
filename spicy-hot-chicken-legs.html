Spicy Hot Chicken Legs

Size: 138,1 kilobytes (138,076 bytes).

The recipe consists of twelve drumsticks (meaty chicken legs), by pouring hot red pepper sauce over the drumsticks, then Seasoning them with cubes of butter, teaspoons of garlic and onion powder, salt and pepper for 3 hours of cooking.

1. Ingredients

  • 12 chicken drumsticks(the meaty leg of chicken).
  • 1(5ounce) bottle hot red pepper sauce
  • 1/4 cup butter, cubed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 and 1/2 cups blue salad dressing

2. Steps

  1. place the drumsticks in a slow cooker and sprinkle with pieces of butter.
  2. pour the hot sauce over the chicken, then
  3. season with garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper.
  4. cover, and cook on high for 3 hours on until tender.
  5. serve the chicken legs with blue cheese dressing on the side.
